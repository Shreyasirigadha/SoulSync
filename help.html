Mental Health Matters

Mental health includes our emotional, psychological, and social well-being. It affects how we think, feel, and act as we cope with life. Mental health is important because it can help you to:

  • Cope with the stresses of life
  • Be physically healthy
  • Have good relationships

Ways to Improve Mental Health

  1. Try to hold on to the positive emotions when you have them, taking a break from negative information.
  2. Taking care of your physical health, since your physical and mental health are connected.
  3. Connecting with others. Having good social support may help protect you against the harms of stress.
  4. Meditation, which is a mind and body practice where you learn to focus your attention and awareness.
  5. Relaxation techniques are practices you do to produce your body's natural relaxation response. This slows down your breathing, lowers your blood pressure, and reduces muscle tension and stress.
